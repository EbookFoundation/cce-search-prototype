If this work has been found to be out of copyright, then it is able to be distributed freely online.


How to determine if a book is in the public domain:

  • Published before 1923: automatically in public domain.
  • Published from 1923-1963: In public domain if copyright hasn’t been renewed.
  • Published from 1964-1977: public domain after 28 years with additional 67 year automatic extension.
  • Published from 1978-present: in public domain for life of author plus additional 70 years.
  • Special circumstances: Created pre 1978 but not published: in public domain for life of author plus additional 70 years OR until 2003, whichever is greater.
